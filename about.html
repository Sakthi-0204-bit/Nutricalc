About the Creator

Hello! My name is Sakthi, the creator of NutriCalc. I'm passionate about promoting healthy living and making nutrition more accessible to everyone. With a background in web development, I have always been fascinated by the science of food and its impact on our well-being.

NutriCalc was born out of my desire to help people make informed choices about their meals. By providing an easy-to-use calorie calculator and comprehensive food descriptions, my goal is to empower you with the knowledge you need to maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Our Mission

At NutriCalc, our mission is simple: to help you understand your food better and make smarter dietary decisions. We believe that health starts with awareness, and we're here to guide you on your journey.
